Welcome to Gun Shot Detection System

How it works?

The system detects gunshots, identifies the specific location of the gunfire, and transmits the information to a small number of selected recipients in real time. The drone is outfitted with omnidirectional microphones placed at strategic positions to obtain the sound signal from all directions. After detecting a gunshot, it triangulates the gunshot source with the time difference of arrival (TDOA) of sound collected by each microphone. Once it locates thegunshot, the drone can use GPS to identify the location of the incident with great precision and provide this information on the map interface.
